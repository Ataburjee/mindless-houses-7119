TRIPPING OUT

Take a Trip with The Row,D.S. & Durga, and Eytys

Whether it’s a weekend sojourn in a new and exciting city, or a long-anticipated three-week-getaway to the Amalfi Coast, we know all too well by now that a trip is about much more than the destination. It’s also a break from your ordinary self—which means that, as necessary as your go-to staples are to include in your suitcase, so is a selection of statement pieces. Here we offer up six different flavors of travel tips, all in the wearable variety.

Featured In This Image: D.S. & DURGA perfume

D.S. & Durga Coriander Eau de Parfum

Infuse your experience (and your memories) with the power of scent. Nothing catalyzes total recall like a favorite fragrance, and D.S. & Durga offer a selection of fragrances that run the gamut from object, to place-inspired. Coriander, with notes of juniper, musk, and clary sage, evokes “the hills above Odessa.”

Model wears The Row dress.

The Row dress.

A garment you can dress up or down is integral to packing light, and this slip-dress from The Row delivers.

Featured In This Image: BAPE travel bag.

Andersson Bell Brown Hand Crochet Messenger Bag

Relax—you’re on vacation! This multicolor crochet bag keeps it real.

Featured In This Image: JACQUES MARIE MAGE sunglasses.

Jacques Marie Mage Black Limited Edition Clyde Sunglasses

With a psychedelic spin on some classic frames, Jacques Marie Mage encourages you to see things a little differently. Who needs rose coloured lenses when you have the power of purple.

Featured In This Image: Eytys sneakers.

Eytys Silver Aphex Low Top Sneakers

Step into something unexpected, in hues of silver and lime green. The best place to test drive a new pair of sneakers in a place where nobody knows your name.

Featured In This Image: Nocs Design earphones.

NCOS Black NS1100 Air Earbuds

Of course, we can’t be immersive all the time. When the soundtrack outside loses its appeal, score your inner world with these earbuds.
